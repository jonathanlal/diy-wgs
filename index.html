The Process

Five phases. The wet lab takes one focused day; the sequencer runs itself.

01

DNA Collection

~15 min

Swish saline mouthwash for 60 seconds, centrifuge the result into a small white cell pellet. No needles, no clinic.

Saline mouthwash Centrifuge 1.5mL tube
02

DNA Extraction

~1.5 hr

Lyse cells with Proteinase K, bind DNA to a silica column, wash away everything else, elute pure genomic DNA. Kit or DIY CTAB method.

Zymo kit Thermocycler Qubit
03

Library Prep

~1 hr

Fragment DNA, repair ends, ligate adapters and motor proteins that pull strands through nanopores. Load onto the flow cell dropwise.

ONT SQK-LSK114 SPRI beads Flow cell
04

Sequencing

72 hr (hands-off)

MinKNOW handles everything. DNA threads through nanopores, current resistance identifies each base. Walk away. Come back in three days.

MinION MinKNOW HAC basecalling
05

Analysis & Dashboard

2–4 hr compute

Align reads to GRCh38, call variants with LongShot, filter to high-confidence SNPs. Then hand everything to Claude for a custom dashboard.

minimap2 LongShot Claude
