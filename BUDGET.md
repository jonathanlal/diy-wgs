## Notes
- Flow cells are the biggest per-genome variable cost ($850 each)
- Better yield per flow cell = fewer needed = biggest lever on cost
- Ligation kit vs Rapid kit is the single biggest yield decision
- Quantifying DNA before loading (Qubit) prevents the most common cause of wasted flow cells
- Subsequent genome costs drop significantly once equipment is purchased
- Flow cells degrade over time — don't stockpile too far ahead
