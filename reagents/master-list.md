## Storage Temperature Reference
| Temp | Items stored here |
|------|------------------|
| Room temp | Zymo wash buffers, elution buffer, PCR tubes, tips |
| 4°C (fridge) | Library Beads (LIB), Flow Cell Flush, BSA, Qubit kits, AMPure beads, flow cells |
| −20°C (freezer) | ONT kit reagents (SQB, FCT, LA, EB), Proteinase K aliquots, DNA aliquots |

⚠️ **Critical:** Flow cells must be stored at 2–8°C. NEVER freeze flow cells — it kills them instantly.

## Reagent Notes
- **g-DNA Wash Buffer**: Add 96 mL 100% ethanol per 24 mL concentrate before first use (check kit insert)
- **80% ethanol**: Prepare fresh — dilute 800 mL 100% ethanol with 200 mL nuclease-free water
- **Proteinase K**: Reconstitute once and aliquot into 100 µL tubes. Each aliquot = 5 extractions.
- **AMPure XP beads**: Let come to room temperature before each use (15–20 min). Vortex well before pipetting.
